The Promised Neverland

"The Promised Neverland" is a horror/fantasy anime. The anime depicts the hardships of the characters who were born into a "child farm". In the anime, the characters end up knowing about their situation and make efforts to escape. They end up meeting several people who help them travel towards the human world.
